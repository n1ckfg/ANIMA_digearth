Once a city was transposed into a cloud. The citizens streamed in white and rain. They had previously existed
as a grain of sand, and managed
a completely ecologically sound existence.

Something came, that they didn't expect and swept
them into the air, and they fell down as
a summer shower
and of course the King sent out his horses
and men, but nothing could be done and the
once great city was lost to the endless sea.

If you watch a person drowning try to perceive
the eyes of these so perfectly clear homeless surrounding
him, welcoming him, and though his body will thrash and he will gnash his teeth
and spit out the salt of the tearful ocean he will come to rest in the arms of his final beloveds.

For in this city of water everyone is your one true love.

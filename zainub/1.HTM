THROUGH THE SOLES OF MY
MOTHERS FEET
Notes from a sound journal and other ruminations.




The working title, (Nomadic Architecture) aptly describes the premise of the project. Nomadic refers to the migration of the Ismaili and Gujerati community from India to Africa and to the Western world.

I define architecture in the broadest terms to include not only built structures but also the total way in which a culture uses time and space in conceptualizing its social existence. This time and space articulation or chronotope of a specific culture addresses how that culture communicates and interacts with itself, and also the wider social and natural world.

ARTIST
The technological juggernaut is an extension of our hands and minds.

Lucinda Atwood - Imagine a drug of electronic immediacy. The NEED for communication, the desire to be plugged in. Who is pushing and why.

Oliver Hockenhull - Mouth full of tongue and consistent wander. Philosophical hypertext rants, agit-prop, and postcards of the metaphysical self. Extensive.

Mike MacDonald - Silent rendering of patient watching. Butterflies fluttering.

David Nandi Odhiambo and Terence Anthony - Black Urban poetry. Images isolated, painted, made uncanny. A tripping click and the upfront life of the working artists.

K. D. Thornton - Consumate use of the Internet as an artistic exploration, discovery and mapping. A new Universe reflecting on the Ancient, the mundane and the digital. One of the most conclusive speakings about the medium, through the medium, on the net.

Zainub Verjee - A webbed sound journal of another home and the close listening to the maternal.

We are exploring how to make gestures that fulfill the forms of our histories and desires.

Tickle Me

Sensitivity out of Context

Laughter required by Humor

Connecting the way

you usually feel

last felt

prefer to feel

to the way you feel Now.

 

Different times build a bridge.

 

Feeling takes structure

with time.

 

Time is a point, of view,

when it is Now or

when it is Then to Then.

 

And the longest time

between two points is

Real Time!

The End of 'End'

When there is no

'End' at all,

Except for the

End of

Growth,

I'm happy with the

Change to a new

Plain allowing

Thought.

 

No end in

Sight, we

Leave the concept of

'End' behind -

Unaware our new

Prize: 'Thought' would

Project through

Now

Using 'Time':

"If 'End' applies to

Growth, can it also apply to

Now?"

Could this happen to

Us? Could 'Time'

Exist somehow?

 

 

Just

Tragic points and

Paranoid thoughts -

Who could think such

Terrible things?

Not me

Certainly, for

I can

Fly, as if I had

Just grown wings!

 

Launched by a swelling

Wave, I leave the

Sea behind; forsaking

'Thought' for

Growth again, I am

Now beside

Time.

 

These colours fall

Below me - hues of

Concepts float

Around.

Their use to me

Goes with them; I'm

Beyond their common

Ground.

 

 

I am light and

Wisp and liquid as I

Blend with

All That Is.

Only 'Thought' can

Tear us from here,

Where we would

Die to live.

 

Truth comes with its

Concepts and

Wakes us with its

Light showing

'End' to be

Real as is imaginary

Flight.

Overview

Greg Garvey is currently Acting Chair of the Department of Design Art at Concordia University.
Professor Garvey holds a Master of Science in Visual Studies from MIT and was a fellow at the Center for Advanced Visual Studies from 1982-85. He has taught 2D and 3D Computer Graphics since 1986 at the following institutions: the Massachusetts College of Art, Northeastern University, the New England School of Art and Design, Endicott College, and since 1991 at the Department of Design Art at Concordia University. His interactive installations have been included in such publications as WIRED Magazine, National Geographic, The New Scientist, and have been exhibited in North American and Europe.

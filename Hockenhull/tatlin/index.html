Tatlin's Monument To The Third International

 

 

 

A modern Pantheon never built for the trace of the imagination that is the first exile from the borders of a yesterday's mortality and the failure of Revolution. A time machine - but history, but utopia and distopia. Extravagance of sprung steel industry and a vision of pure solids clock worked, co-ordinated, by a solar regulation to run the State. Sometimes only the impossible is worth considering.

minimally a 1 to 2 page written synopsis and treatment is required that describes the work that you propose to build. this can be submitted as a text file or an html file. although it is not required, please feel free to include a limited number of images. it would be useful if you could include the URL for any current web work as well. please also include a 50 word biography which describes your artistic practice.

although the submission deadline is january 1st, 1997, we would like you to send us an intention to participate if you are interested in submitting work. we welcome submission entries and ideas prior to the deadline.

critria


a peer jury including the curators, thecla schiphorst and oliver hockenhull will review the submissions and select the work to be exhibited. this call is open globally to all artists who wish to participate. we will select 9 works in all to participate in transverse worlds.





hnoraria

the canada council of the Arts has enabled us to provide honoraria of $400 cdn to be awarded to four selected canadian artists.

In addition we will invite up to 5 outstanding works selected from all the applications.

ntwrk spport


all works selected will be provided with server space and access and technical network support. no financial resources (other than the already stated honorariums) are available through transverse worlds at this time.

selected artists must have their own local technical support for constructing their work, and provide transverse worlds with a completed, web-ready, up-loadable piece by may 1st 1997.

work that has been selected but has not been completed by may 1, 1997 will not be included in the opening launch which will occur may 15th to 18th during veaf '97, the third annual vancouver electronic arts festival.

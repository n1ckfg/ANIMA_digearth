Digital eARTh presents DEFT >> Digital/Electronic Festival of Art and Technology March 31 - May 31, 2000 Vancouver, B.C.

Zero Degree Monstrosity: Biotech/Immortality/Evolution

DEFT 2000 DEFT 2000 is a digital arts festival to be held in Vancouver, Canada.

With its theme of Zero Degree Monstrosity: biotech/immortality/ evolution, DEFT 2000 will be an opportunity to examine the role of bioengineering in art and society. This event is a challenge to understand the mutations created by technology and its effects on biovolution. With DEFT 2000, Digital eARTh envisions a city wide look ahead to the third millenium through the lens of digital art and biotechnology. How is our world being altered by developments in the lifesciences? What is the role of the artists within the interplay of science and society?

Zero Degree Monstrosity: Biotech/Immortality/Evolution

On the threshold of the new millennium, the notion of the human body rests upon an image that is intrinsically prescriptive: a normally formed human is Zero Degree Monstrosity (ZDM). ZDM is the ground zero of an assumed humanity which has never been met but is continually repromised as a heavenly immortality bounded tight in a world thoroughly calculated, precise, and ideal. Technological changes are causing alterations in the nature of our senses, our environment and ultimately our corporeality.

The human, being so profoundly a social creature is most dependent upon its placement with the cultural codes of its society. Digital, genetic, telecommunications, drugs and organ farms, cloning factories, the software that runs the human program, the human machine called civilization are all experiencing the permanent revolution - foot to the floor technological acceleration. Perpetual mutation is the resultant evolutionary grimace. Based upon the achievements of digital information technology, biotechnology and genetic engineering are bringing forth a new definition of our future selves. The new human being is preselected following assumed norms: is this an ideal or a monster?

The cultural dynamics of ZDM is a distribution of power and capital that often erases excess and mutation yet monsters are the primary artifact of evolution signage. What light does monstrosity throw on immortality and evolution? What is our current conception of what it means to be human? Are we all now monsters of the new world disorder? Will the digital revolution be followed by a biological one?

DEFT 2000 deploys an interweaving of the theoretical-scholarly and artistic modes of confronting issues. Along with a symposium which constitutes the core of Zero Degree Monstrosities: biotech/immortality/ evolution, a diverse array of artists, critics and scientists will confront and deal with the cultural-philosophical, social-political, and metaphysical issues raised by genetic engineering. It is necessary, at this juncture in time, that a focused context for discussion is engendered which allows for clarity without simplification and openness without the loss of criticality.

Affiliations and Partnerships

Zero Degree Monstrosity: Biotech/Immortality/Evolution will attempt to make this discourse accessible through exhibitions of new media, film/video screenings, lectures and a symposium. DEFT 2000 will provide the umbrella for events including organization and operations, publicity and a symposium. The festival can only have breath with the participation of affiliations among art centres, cultural institutions and community participation. We invited you to engage with the theme of the Digital/ Electronic Festival of Art and Technology by programming events within your respective organization during the festival run. DEFT 2000 will provide organizational support, networking and publicity for the event as a whole. As Digital eARTh is a virtual society, we will require space to exhibit new media work, offer workshops, screen film/video and showcase guest lectures and presentations.

Symposium

Zero Degree Monstrosity: Biotech/Immortality/Evolution will be the focused theme of the symposium. Over the course of three days, DEFT 2000 will organize a series of panel discussions and papers addressing key themes. International speakers will sit alongside local artists, academics and critics to evaluate the important issues that will permeate the next millennium. We plan to pose critical questions and generate debate about the future rather than reevaluate the past.

Aims and Intentions

The intention of DEFT 2000 and Zero Degree Monstrosity: Biotech/Immortality/Evolution is to evaluate the role of art in the realm of biotechnology and to foster an understanding of art's role as a decisive factor in social transformation. The challenges wrought by genetic engineering effect both society and an engaged art practice. DEFT 2000 envisions the examination of biotechnology and digital culture in the form of a variety of events, installations, performances, exhibitions, and network projects. We encourage you to participate in whatever capacity you might envision. Your contributions, questions and input are welcome, indeed we anticipate them. DEFT 2000 aims to encompass as many viewpoints as there are participants. The festival is designed to facilitate discussion, broaden awareness and encourage new audiences with respect to the future of biotechnology and digital culture.

Digital Earth

Digital earth makes the widening cultural promises of technology real by making visible the web that unites our diverse communities.

Digital earth is a response to the growing interest within the community concerning the possibilities of greater connectivity between peoples, issues, organizations, media, and the arts through the use of the new technologies.

Digital earth's visionary objective is based on an appreciation of the patterns that connect, an ecological and evolutionary awareness of the joint venture that is communications, community, and culture.

Developments in technology, in interactivity and in tele-communications allow us the venue of the virtual, a no-place, a utopia, that can sponsor the realm of our imagination. To become responsible for our imagination we must become sufficiently aware to support through opportunity and initiative the network of learning about our many cultural selves.
